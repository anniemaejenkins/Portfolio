Who is Annie, anyways?

Since I first picked up a controller to play games, I have always had an interest in technology. I got into art at an early age and continue to pursue it as a fun hobby when I have downtime. When I discovered blogging on the web as a middle schooler, I became obsessed by code and how I could use it to change the way my layout and words would appear on my page. When I reached out to learn more, I was steered away from continuing and focused my time into art. When I saw the opportunity to give code another chance, I decided to try to see what all I could do and have enjoyed the challenges and successes at my time in The Iron Yard.
